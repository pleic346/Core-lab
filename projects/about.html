STATEMENT OF PURPOSE

The purpose of this website is for it to be an informative yet interactive platform in which you can learn about the different piers that surround New York; its many views, charming restaurants, and hang out places. The main goal being for the viewer to experience a ‘run’ through the pier.

TARGET AUDIENCE

The intended audience for the websites are runners! Locals who run at the piers daily and want to know more about the places they can stop at and discover. It is also meant for tourists who want to walk along the edge of New York enjoying the jaw-dropping view of the skyline meeting the river. Furthermore, the website can also be navigated by locals who have never been to the pier and decide to take a walk away from the busy streets.
